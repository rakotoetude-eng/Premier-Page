Tononkalo

Ny aina

        Tora-javona maraina,
        Setroka mihalohalo,
        Ranon'ando mora maina,
        tsy mitoetra fa mandalo
    
        Maro zavatra mifofo,
        Toa bakoly mora vaky,
        Voninkazo mora miofo
        'Zay hihintsana malaky
    
        Tsy mba mafy fa makiana
        Toa ny raty na zozoro,
        Trano tonta tsy odiana
        Ka tsy ela dia mikoro.
    
        Volomborona milefa,
        Entin-drivotra mitsoka,
        Volana mirefarefa,
        Mbetivety dia hisoka.
    
        Tsy azo tanana hitoetra
        Toy ny fofon'ny nahandro.
        Raha alina no fetra
        Dia tsy tratry ny masoandro.
    
        Tsy mba fantatra hiaingana
        Fa mitsaika tsy ahina,
        Voron-kely tsy tamana
        Ka mikopaka mangina.
    
        Mpivahiny tonga omaly,
        Tsy mba honina ela akory,
        Fony tonga nampifaly,
        Nony lasa mampahory.
    
        Io no aina tsy mba roa
        Ka tandremanantsika ery,
        Tsy voavidin-java-tsoa
        Sady maro mpankafy.
    
        "Mamy" fa tsy vazivazy,
        Eny, tena sarobidy;
        Nefa izay fitiavana azy
        Tsy handaozako ny adidy!

J.Narivony
